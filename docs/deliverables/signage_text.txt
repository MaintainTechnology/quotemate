WHAT IS THIS DOCUMENT?
This Document sets out details of your obligations as a F45 Franchisee, and provides information that is referred to in your Agreement and the
Operations Manual. In particular, this Document sets out important information regarding the installation and overall appearance of your Studio. 
UPDATES TO THIS DOCUMENT 
This Document will be updated from time to time as the need arises. We encourage you to keep up to date with the policies and procedures on the F45 Intranet, 
which are accessible via F45Playbook.com. Where we make an amendment or otherwise update the requirements set out in this Document, we will provide 
you with a reasonable amount of time to effect any changes required for your Studio. 
POLICIES AND GUIDANCE 
Parts of this Document provides guidance, while other parts set out policies and procedures that you are required to follow as part of your Agreement with F45. 
If the Document says you must do something or uses similar words, this is a requirement rather than a guideline, and you are expected to comply with 
the requirement. Failure to do so may be a breach of your Agreement, which if not remedied, could lead to the termination of the Agreement. 
YOUR OBLIGATIONS 
You have various obligations under the terms of your Agreement, this Document and other policies and procedures introduced by F45 from time to
time. You must familiarise yourself with your obligations under each of those documents.
We reserve the right to ask you for information, documents and or receipt to verify your compliance with your obligations under this Document or your Agreement. 
If requested, within the time specified (if any) or, if no time is specified, as soon as possible.
If you have failed to comply with your obligations under the Agreement or this Document, we may request you to do anything or act which we believe is necessary 
(acting reasonably) to comply with your obligations. 
COMPLIANCE WITH THIS DOCUMENT 
You must strictly observe the requirements set out in this Document, unless you obtain the prior written approval of F45. Any deviation from F45 HQ Global Signage
standards may result in a breach of your Franchise Agreement until such time that your signage is brought into compliance with F45 HQ standards.

F45 has strict signage requirements that relate to:
Internal signage, that is the signage that appears inside your Studio (Internal Signage);
External signage, that is signage that appears outside of your Studio including motor vehicles and alike (External Signage).
These requirements are set out in this Document.
FOLLOWING DIRECTIONS FROM F45 
Your Internal Signage and External Signage will vary depending upon the set up and layout of your Studio. F45 has the right, at its absolute discretion,
to determine the design, layout and the type of signage that needs to be installed in your Studio.
You will be required to submit to F45 your proposal for Internal Signage and External Signage for approval. You must follow any directions or requests
given to you by F45 in relation to the modification of your proposals. 
SIGNAGE APPROVAL 
All signage inside and outside of your Studio must be approved by F45. 
Once you have obtained this approval you must not alter or modify the signage in any manner and must ensure that the installation of the
signage strictly accords with the terms of the approval from F45. If your signage does not accord with that approval, your Studio will not be approved
to open. 
USE A PROFESSIONAL 
We highly recommend that you use a professional signage installer for your Internal and External Signage.
SOURCING OF SIGNAGE 
You are responsible for sourcing (and paying for) your Internal and External Signage, as these items are not supplied by F45. Please note that the equipment pack may
include the legacy F45 internal signage. If you receive the legacy internal signage designs, please do not install this signage within your studio as it is no longer
compliant. You may dispose of these decals at your discretion. Refunds or credits related to the previous internal signage decals will not apply.

For both internal and external signage, you must engage the services of a professional signage installer, as you are required to source this signage separately from the

INTERNAL SIGNAGE PROCESS 
Once your site has been approved, you may submit your proposal for internal signage to F45 HQ at any time, however we recommend that you wait until
your floorplan has been approved before you submit any signage proposal. Your proposal for internal signage must:
be in a single document (preferably a PDF);
show the walls for which the signage is to be installed on (in a photograph), with the proposed signage overlaid on top of the relevant image;
show the measurements for the signage so F45 HQ can verify that the internal signage will be installed in accordance with F45 HQ specifications;
include any additional details requested by F45 HQ from time to time.
EXTERNAL SIGNAGE
You may submit your proposal for external signage as soon as your site has been approved by F45 HQ.
Your proposal for external signage must:
be in a single document (preferably PDF);
show the exterior surface of the Premises (in a photograph), with the proposed signage overlaid on top of the relevant image;
show the measurements for the signage so F45 HQ can verify that the external signage will be installed in accordance with F45 HQ specifications; and
include any additional details requested by F45 HQ from time to time.
MODIFICATIONS OR ALTERATIONS TO SIGNAGE
Once your signage has been installed you must not alter or modify the signage or install additional signage without prior written approval from F45 HQ.

Step 1 - Review the window wrap/ poster/ A-frame options available on pages 29-33 in these Guidelines. Choose which images
you would like to use for your window wraps.
Step 2 - Once the decision has been made above, download the selected high-res image(s) from the “High-Res Image Folder." in
Playbook under Studio Signage Files.
Step 3 - Download the Adobe Illustrator file named "Window Wrap Text Templates" (either with an image or without, depending
on your preference.
