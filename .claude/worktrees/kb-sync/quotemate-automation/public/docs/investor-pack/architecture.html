Each Stage In One Line

Onboard 1
Tradie signs up — via the website OR by texting the shared QuoteMate number and getting a personal link back.
Onboard 2
Fills the tradie form — business name, trades (electrical / plumbing / both), per-trade hourly rate, markup, callout, licence body, ABN.
Onboard 3
Hits Activate. One tap — no manual steps after this.
Onboard 4
System auto-provisions in the background — Twilio buys a real AU number, Vapi spins up a voice assistant, welcome SMS lands on the tradie's phone.
Onboard 5
Receptionist Live — the AU number on the tradie's site is now answered by the AI 24/7. This is the moment the runtime flow below becomes available.
Stage 01
Customer texts, calls, or sends an MMS photo to the tradie's number.
Stage 02
Twilio receives it on the per-tradie AU number and webhooks our app.
Stage 03
SMS goes to the dialog agent (Haiku 4.5). Voice goes to Vapi (Haiku 4.5, parked behind env flag).
Stage 04
Opus 4.7 reads the full transcript + photos and writes a clean structured job brief.
Stage 05
Opus 4.7 drafts the quote, looking up real prices from the tradie's pricing book, the shared catalogue, and similar past quotes.
Stage 06
Three tiers come out — Good, Better, Best — every price grounded in real catalogue data.
Stage 07
Customer gets a quote SMS + mobile quote page link. Tradie sees a new card on the dashboard.
Stage 08
Customer pays a deposit via Stripe Checkout. Money currently lands in the QuoteMate account; Stripe Connect flips on when tradie #2 onboards.
Stage 09
Customer picks a slot. Both sides get a confirmation SMS.
