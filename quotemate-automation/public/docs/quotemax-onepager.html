05 / The setup

The three-minute wizard.

Four screens take a tradie from sign-up to a live quoting line. The catalogue is the make-or-break step, so we make it effortless — pick from a pre-built library for their trade, not a blank page.

1

Business & preferences

Name, ABN, licence, service area and brand voice — what dresses every quote so it goes out looking like their business, not ours.

2

Build the catalogue

Start from a ready-made assembly library for their trade and just toggle what they offer. No spreadsheets, no blank page.

The critical step
3

Set the rates

Hourly rate, call-out minimum, markup. Every quote prices itself off their numbers — the AI never invents a price.

4

Go live with the number

A dedicated Australian number to hand out — on the van, the website, the card. The moment it’s live, the AI is answering.
