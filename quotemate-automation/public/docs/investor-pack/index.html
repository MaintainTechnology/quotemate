Why This Wins

Narrow wedge, deep moat

Two trades, two cities, five job types each. Every dollar in every quote comes from a real pricing book the tradie helped build — not hallucinated by an LLM.

Tradie in the loop

No auto-send in v1. Australian Consumer Law treats accepted quotes as binding contracts. The tradie reviews every draft before it goes — that's the liability shield and the trust builder.

Portal-first, not voice-first

Voice agents cost ~$1,500/month per tradie at moderate call volume. SMS at SaaS margins works. Voice is a premium upsell when the unit economics make sense.

Real money, real tradies

Live Twilio numbers, live Vapi assistants, live Stripe Checkout. Electrical pilot underway in NSW, plumbing pilot underway in Brisbane.
