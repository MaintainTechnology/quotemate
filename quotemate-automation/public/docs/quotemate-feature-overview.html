Quotes that sell

Money, sorted

Money is handled end-to-end — site-visit fees, deposits, the final payment and the processing fees — so the tradie keeps their full rate and just does the work.

07

Deposit locks the job

A deposit is paid up front to lock the job in before any work starts.

→ Money down means committed, not just "thinking about it."

→ Quoting visits stop being free labour.

08

→ No chasing invoices weeks later.
