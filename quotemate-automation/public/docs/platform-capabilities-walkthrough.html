01

VOICE INTAKE

Ring, talk to the AI, get a quote by SMS.

Intake channelAfter-hours ready
What it does

When a customer calls your number, an AI receptionist (voice name Jon) answers and asks about the job: where it is, what needs doing, and when they need it. The call is turned into a tidy job summary, and a quote is drafted and sent back to the customer by SMS within a couple of minutes. No email, no forms, no hold music.

Why it helps you

You stay out of the office. Calls come through after hours and on weekends, the AI handles the intake without bothering you, and the quote lands automatically. Less time on the phone, more jobs quoted.

How it works
  1. Customer rings the number you have been given.
  2. Jon answers, greets them, and asks what they need and where.
  3. Jon takes their name, suburb, job type and urgency, then reads the summary back to confirm.
  4. The call ends and the chat is turned into a structured job (details, access, risks).
  5. QuoteMax drafts a quote and texts it to the customer within 2 minutes.

02

SMS INTAKE

An AI receptionist that texts back and sends the quote link.

Intake channelPhotos supported
What it does

A customer texts your number. The AI runs a normal back-and-forth conversation to get the details: name, suburb, what needs doing, and the specifics. It can ask for a photo too. Once it has enough, it drafts the quote and texts the customer a link to view their quote and price.

Why it helps you

You stay out of the text thread completely. No typing, no missed messages. Customers get an instant quote without a form, and the AI sorts out any questions for you. Nothing slips through the cracks.

How it works
  1. Customer texts your registered SMS number with a job.
  2. The AI confirms their name and asks about the job (type, suburb, scope).
  3. It keeps going until it has what it needs, usually 3 to 5 messages.
  4. It sends a photo upload link when a picture would help (e.g. downlights, drains).
  5. It drafts the quote and texts a link to view the quote.

Job types it can quote on the spot include downlights, power points, ceiling fans, smoke alarms, blocked drains, hot water, taps and toilets. Tricky jobs (asbestos, switchboard work, burst pipes, bathroom renos) are routed to a paid on-site inspection instead of an instant quote.

03

AI QUOTE ESTIMATOR

Electrical & plumbing prices, straight from your rate book.

Auto-sends when confidentOne clear price
What it does

This is the engine behind your quotes. It builds one clear, priced quote for electrical and plumbing jobs. Every price must come from your rate book, your assemblies, materials and hourly rate. The AI is not allowed to make a number up. If it can't price something properly, the whole quote drops to a paid inspection instead.

Why it helps you

The same job is priced the same way every time, so there is no guesswork and no pricing drift. Confident quotes go straight to the customer. You get consistent, defensible numbers that build trust.

How it works
  1. The job details come in (type, scope, how many, and the specifics).
  2. The AI looks up your real assemblies and materials for that trade.
  3. It builds your quote using your prices and your hourly rate, never a free-typed number.
  4. A checker confirms every line traces back to your rate book and meets your minimum labour.
  5. Clean quotes auto-send. Anything that fails the check becomes a $99 inspection.

04

ROOFING

Type an address, measure the roof, get a price.

You review before sendMeasured from satellite
What it does

Enter a property address and the tool measures every building on the block, the house plus any sheds or garages, from satellite data. It works out the sloped roof area, the hips and valleys, and the roof shape. Then it prices the job from your rates, ready for you to review and send.

Why it helps you

No more measuring off Google Maps or climbing up for a rough idea. You get an instant area and a solid price baseline, so you can focus on the call you actually make on site (material and complexity). Every roofing quote waits for your sign-off before it goes out.

How it works
  1. Enter the property address (suburb, postcode, state).
  2. Set the material, the pitch, and what the job is (re-roof, leak, re-bed ridges).
  3. The tool measures all structures and shows area, storeys, hips and valleys.
  4. A price appears per building, plus a combined total.
  5. You review, toggle buildings in or out, then send as a customer quote.

PAINTING

An instant repaint ballpark from the property size.

Residential auto-quoteInterior & exterior
What it does

Type an address and pick what you want painted: interior walls, ceilings, trim, or exterior. The tool estimates the floor area from property data, works out the paintable square metres, and gives you a price with a confidence band. Low-confidence jobs are flagged for an on-site measure.

Why it helps you

Painting quotes normally need a site visit. This gives you an instant ballpark so you can decide if a job is worth driving out for, and it keeps your per-square-metre rates consistent across every quote.

AIR CONDITIONING

Size and price the right system from the home's details.

Always books a site visitSized by room
What it does

Enter the home's address, the number of bedrooms and living areas, the floor size, ceiling height and insulation. The tool works out the cooling load for each room, sizes the right system (split or ducted), and shows the inc-GST price from your rate card. Every result finishes by booking a site assessment.

Why it helps you

Kills the guesswork on split vs ducted, and gives the customer a clear, itemised price. It also flags the tricky bits (raked ceilings, high load, tight budget) up front, so you know what to scope when you get there.

How it works
  1. Enter the address (postcode sets the climate zone) and home details.
  2. The engine calculates the cooling load room by room.
  3. It sizes and prices the right system for the home.
  4. You see a price range, a best-fit flag, and a booking button.
  5. The customer books a site assessment to confirm and lock it in.

SOLAR

Size, output and price from the roof, rebate included.

Auto-sends when cleanSTC rebate built in
What it does

Enter a home's address and the tool reads the roof from satellite imagery to find the area and which way it faces. It sizes the right system for the roof, works out the yearly power output and the STC rebate, and shows the net price after rebate, inc GST. Clean estimates auto-send to the customer. Anything outside the safe limits waits for your review.

Why it helps you

No rooftop measuring and no spreadsheet sizing. A single address gives a complete quote with a clear cost breakdown (gross, rebate, net) and realistic output figures. It respects export limits and sends tricky roofs to an inspection.

How it works
  1. Enter the address. Satellite imagery finds the roof area and tilt.
  2. It sizes the right system to fit the roof and export limit.
  3. It works out the STC rebate from the postcode and the system size.
  4. Safety checks run on price, payback and output. Odd ones get flagged.
  5. Clean estimates auto-send with a PDF and SMS. Flagged ones wait for you.

CUSTOMER QUOTE & BOOKING

What the customer sees, accepts, pays and books, all on their phone.

Customer viewDeposit & calendar
What it does

When a customer taps the SMS link, they land on a clean quote page showing their price with an indicative photo. They accept it, pay a small refundable deposit by card, choose a time from your calendar, and get a confirmation, all on their phone. Jobs that need an inspection show a $99 refundable site-visit deposit instead.

Why it helps you

It takes the friction out of saying yes. Customers see pricing straight away and book themselves with no back-and-forth. You get a deposit up front while keeping their commitment low (30%, fully refunded if they cancel). One clear price means no confusion and no haggling.

How it works
  1. Customer taps the SMS link and sees their job, scope and price.
  2. They read the quote and tap to accept.
  3. They pay the 30% deposit by card (secure checkout).
  4. They pick a time from your available slots and confirm.
  5. They get a confirmation SMS. You are notified and confirm the day before.
